Bachelor of Engineering [BE] (Civil Engineering)


BE Civil Engineering is a 4 year undergraduate engineering degree course.
This course deals with the construction, design and maintenance of structures such as bridges, canals, tunnels, buildings, waterworks, airports etc.
Students must have cleared their 10+2 level examinations with physics, chemistry and mathematics subjects for admission to the BE Civil Engineering course.
