This course includes computer programming, software, operating system, and computer hardware etc.
Candidates wanting to pursue BE CSE have to clear their 10+2 in Science.
Further, they are required to sit for the entrance examination like JEE Main, WBJEE, TNEA and CUCET etc. and qualify it.

Followings are some of the important points regarding admission in a good BE CSE college.
  • The admission of the candidates will depend upon the score obtained in the entrance examination. So it is very much necessary for the candidates to secure a good CGPA for admission in a good college.
  • The aspiring candidates should research about the various colleges before applying just to make sure that the college has acceptable infrastructure to provide the course.
  • An ideal college must have sufficient faculties for the course to provide quality education along with ultimate assistance.
  • A good BE CSE College must have a developed Placement Wing including Internship opportunity to provide job opportunities to the deserving candidates.
