Bachelor of Engineering [BE] (Electrical and Electronics Engineering)


B.E. Electrical and Electronics Engineering is a 4-year under-graduation level degree aimed towards imparting knowledge and applying the concepts of electrical and electronics engineering to solve industry-related problems and issues.
The eligibility criteria required for the B.E.
EEE course is that the interested candidate must pass 10+2 Boards with Science as the major discipline with at least 45%-50% marks from any well-known and recognized Institute or Board or any of its equivalent.
The admission process is done through Online and Offline mode.
Admission is either Merit-based or Entrance-Based, it varies according to the institute.
The course is completed after undergoing 8 different semesters.

Entrance-Exam Based Admissions
  • The interested candidates will be required to sit for the entrance exam.
  • He/she must be accompanied with the required documents and essentials as prescribed or instructed.
  • Once, the candidate qualifies for the entrance exam after obtaining a valid score, a merit list of selected students is released.
  • The selected students are invited for the counseling round, Personal Interview Round, or Group Discussions (if any).
