Sri Jayachamarajendra College of Engineering is a private Institute established in 1963 and affiliated with VTU Belgaum. The College has been approved by AICTE and NBA and has been ranked at #151 by NIRF 2019. The campus is spread over 117 acres of area.
SJCE is known for its flagship BE course and admission is strictly based on inter-se merit of the qualifying exam. The Institute accepts a valid KCET score followed by counselling rounds for BE admission. The KCET 2021 is scheduled to be conducted between August 28, 2021 and August 30, 2021.
Candidates who wish to apply for MBA and MCA programs must have a valid PGCET score. The mode of application is both Online and Offline. Check SCJE Admission & Selection Criteria
SCJE provides internship opportunities to students every year. M.Tech students are offered around 8 weeks of Industrial training followed by project assignments. The Internship stipend ranges between INR 15,000/- per month to INR 35,000/- per month. The placement record of the college is also quite good. The average salary package offered is INR 5 LPA.
